Login Instructions

Rajasthan Royals Academy Melbourne — Player DNA Profile

1 Open the App

Navigate to the DNA Profile app using the link provided by your coach or program coordinator.

2 Enter Your Credentials

On the login screen, enter the Username and Password provided to you by your coach.

  • Your username is typically your first name and last initial (e.g., johns).
  • Usernames are all lowercase with no spaces.
  • Use the eye icon next to the password field to reveal your password if needed.

3 Sign In

Tap the SIGN IN button. You will be taken to your Player Portal where you can:

  • View your recent training sessions
  • Write journal entries to reflect on sessions
  • Track your Individual Development Plan (IDP) goals

4 New Players — Register

If you don't have an account yet, tap New player? Register here on the login screen to create one. You will need:

  • Your full name
  • A username (lowercase, no spaces)
  • A password (minimum 6 characters)

After registering, you'll be guided through the onboarding process to build your player DNA profile.

Trouble logging in? Double-check your username is lowercase with no spaces. If you've forgotten your password, contact your program coordinator for a reset.
